# Introduction to Geology

Geology is the scientific study of the Earth, its materials, and the processes that act upon them. It includes the study of rocks, minerals, fossils, and the history of the Earth itself.

## Rock Types

There are three main types of rocks:

*   **Igneous rocks** are formed from the cooling and solidification of magma or lava.
*   **Sedimentary rocks** are formed from the accumulation and cementation of mineral and organic particles.
*   **Metamorphic rocks** are formed from the transformation of existing rock types through heat, pressure, or chemical reactions.
